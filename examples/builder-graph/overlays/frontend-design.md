## Marshmallow Alignment

### Preserve

- Preserve responsive layout, accessibility, and interaction-state checks.

### Override Defaults

- Prefer calm hierarchy and conversational warmth over dashboard density.

### Quality Bar

- The interface should feel helper-like, restrained, and legible.

### Anti-Patterns

- Avoid decorative glass cards, excessive rounding, and gradients without hierarchy.

### Ask When

- Ask when the task genuinely requires dense operational controls.

### Source Trail

- `helper-not-dashboard` - defines the emotional contract.
- `avoid-decorative-glass` - names the rejected default.
- `warm-interface-reference` - supplies the quality anchor.
