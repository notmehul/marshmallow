# Avoid Decorative Glass

## Rule

Use hierarchy and product focus before decorative translucency, heavy rounding,
or gradients.

## Evidence

- `reject-glass-dashboard` - the rejected reference illustrates decorative softness replacing hierarchy.

## Use In Skills

- `frontend-design` - critique decorative surfaces when they weaken scanning or
  decision-making.

## Limits

This does not ban expressive visuals; it rejects decoration that substitutes for
structure.

## Connections

- [[helper-not-dashboard]] - both nodes steer interfaces away from ornamental
  control-panel density.
