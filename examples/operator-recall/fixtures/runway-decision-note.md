# Runway Decision Note

Loomline decided not to raise in June 2026. The team has 14 months of runway,
retention is improving but not yet strong enough to support a credible growth
round, and the next six weeks should focus on proving repeat usage from design
partners.

The accepted tradeoff is slower hiring. The benefit is a cleaner fundraising
story if July and August retention cohorts keep improving. The decision should
be revisited when four consecutive weekly cohorts show repeat usage above the
target threshold.
