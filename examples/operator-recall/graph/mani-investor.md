# Mani Investor

## Current Model

Mani prefers short, direct updates. Lead with the ask, explain what changed, and
show the evidence behind any strategic choice. He is comfortable with bad news
when the reasoning is clear.

## Evidence

- `investor-format-note` - Mani prefers a clear ask, a concise change summary,
  and evidence behind strategic choices; he dislikes inflated momentum claims
  that bury the real constraint.

## Use In Work

- Do not over-polish constraints.
- Include what changed since the last update and which decision needs judgment.

## Limits

Do not invent an ask if the task only needs decision recall.
