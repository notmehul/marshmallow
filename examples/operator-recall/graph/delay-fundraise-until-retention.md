# Delay Fundraise Until Retention

## Current Model

Loomline decided not to raise in June 2026. The company has enough runway to
wait, and the current priority is proving repeat usage before asking investors
to underwrite growth.

## Evidence

- `runway-decision-note` - the team has 14 months of runway, retention is
  improving but not strong enough for a credible growth round, and the next six
  weeks should focus on repeat usage from design partners.

## Use In Work

- Explain the decision as deliberate sequencing.
- Name the tradeoff: slower hiring now for a cleaner fundraising story later.
- Revisit when four consecutive weekly cohorts show repeat usage above target.

## Limits

Do not treat this as permanent fundraising strategy. It is tied to June 2026
runway and retention evidence.
