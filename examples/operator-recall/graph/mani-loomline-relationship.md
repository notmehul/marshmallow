# Mani Loomline Relationship

## Current Model

The useful dynamic is not hype. Mani should be given the real constraint,
current evidence, and a focused ask: does the retention threshold make the
fundraising revisit credible?

## Evidence

- `investor-format-note` - Mani wants concise updates with the ask, what
  changed, and evidence behind strategic choices.
- `runway-decision-note` - the open strategic question is whether the next
  cohorts can support a stronger round later.

## Use In Work

- Ask for judgment on the revisit threshold, not generic fundraising advice.
- Keep the message short and direct.

## Limits

Do not imply Mani has approved the strategy; the current context only supports
asking for his judgment.
